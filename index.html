Xero Commands List

      Profile

      Edit your profile to show off to your friends!

      Command
      Description
      /profile set-bio [String]
      Update your bio (This will be shown everywhere that references you, like /work)
      /profile remove-bio
      Delete your bio because you are an OG
      /profile view [User (Optional)]
      View your (Or someone else's) profile!

      Economy

      Xero's Economy is comprised of Credits (‚å¨). You can get a job and work every so often to earn more Credits and buy items from the store! If you are in a Faction, 10% of your income will go to the Faction's total Credits.

      Command
      Description
      /jobs
      View the Job list
      /baltop
      View the top 10 richest people on Xero!
      /work
      Work to earn money (If you have a job)
      /shop
      View the shop and buy items!
      /inventory
      View your items
      /transfer [Amount] [User]
      Transfer your credits to another user
      /gamble [Amount]
      Gamble your life away!

      Misc

      Command
      Description
      /help
      Get help with all things Xero!
      /math [Equation]
      Solve basic equations (No Variables)
      /bubble
      Play with bubble wrap!
      /splash
      Receive a random splash text
      /minecraft [Ip]
      Ping a Minecraft Server
      /embed [Title] [Description] [Footer]
      Create a custom Rich Embed!
      /poll [Title] [Description]
      Run a poll (Provided you have set a Poll Channel in /settings)
